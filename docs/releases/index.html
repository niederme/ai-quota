AIQuota 1.9.13

Published April 12, 2026
  • Adaptive Auto refresh now speeds up to 1 minute when AIQuota is active or quota is near a threshold, then backs off automatically when your Mac is idle, offline, or in Low Power Mode.
  • Onboarding now includes a refresh preference so you can choose how often AIQuota stays up to date from the start.
  • Archived app builds launch more reliably after being moved into Applications.
