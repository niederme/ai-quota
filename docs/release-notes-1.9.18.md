## What's New in 1.9.18

- **Monthly overage spend at a glance** — AIQuota now shows how much Codex bonus credit spend and Claude extra usage you have used this month.
- **Clearer credit details** — Codex balances are shown as dollars, separate from the monthly amount spent after limits.
- **Cleaner overage styling** — Spent rows are highlighted in amber across the popover and widgets, with red reserved for true limit or cap states.
- **Helpful spend context** — Hover over the Spent row in the menu bar popover to see how the monthly amount is calculated and when it applies.
